Healthy Homes Consultancy & Upgrade

Our healthy homes consultancy and upgrade services are designed to help property owners and managers create healthier living environments for occupants. We offer a comprehensive range of solutions to assess, improve, and maintain the health and well-being of urban homes.

Our Healthy Homes Consultancy and Upgrade Services Include:

  • Indoor Air Quality Assessments: We conduct thorough assessments of indoor air quality to identify pollutants, allergens, and other factors that may impact occupant health.
  • Mold and Moisture Control: Solutions to address mold and moisture issues, including remediation strategies and preventive measures to maintain a healthy indoor environment.
  • Ventilation Improvements: Design and implementation of ventilation systems to enhance air circulation and reduce the buildup of indoor pollutants.
  • Allergen Reduction: Strategies to minimize allergens in the home, such as dust mites, pet dander, and pollen, to improve respiratory health.
  • Healthy Building Materials: Recommendations for using non-toxic and sustainable building materials that promote a healthier living environment.
  • Energy Efficiency Upgrades: Integration of energy-efficient solutions that not only reduce energy costs but also contribute to a healthier home environment.
  • Healthy Home Certifications: Assistance with achieving certifications that recognize healthy home practices, such as WELL Building Standard and Fitwel.
  • Healthy Home Consulting: Expert advice on best practices for maintaining a healthy home, including lifestyle changes, cleaning routines, and maintenance tips.
  • Healthy Home Upgrades: Tailored upgrade solutions to enhance the health and well-being of occupants, such as air purifiers, humidity control systems, and non-toxic furnishings.
  • Healthy Lighting Upgrades: Partnering with RAY lighting we can assess and upgrade LED lighting to help reduce blue spectrum cellular stress and replace lighting systems with healthier full spectrum light closer to the natural full spectrum light we recieve from the sun.

By implementing our healthy homes consultancy and upgrade services, property owners can create safer, more comfortable living environments that promote the health and well-being of occupants. Contact us today to learn how we can help you create a healthier home!
