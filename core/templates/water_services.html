Water Services

Our water services focus on optimizing water usage and promoting sustainable water management practices in urban environments. We offer a range of solutions to help cities reduce water waste, improve efficiency, and enhance resilience against water-related challenges.

Our Water Services Include:

  • Water Audits: Comprehensive assessments of water usage in buildings and infrastructure to identify inefficiencies and opportunities for conservation.
  • Leak Detection: Advanced technologies to detect and address leaks in water systems, reducing waste and preventing damage.
  • Rainwater Harvesting: Design and implementation of systems to capture and utilize rainwater for non-potable uses, reducing reliance on municipal water supplies.
  • Greywater Recycling: Solutions for treating and reusing greywater from sinks, showers, and laundry for irrigation and other non-potable applications.
  • Water Efficiency Consulting: Expert advice on best practices for water conservation, including fixture upgrades, behavioral changes, and policy recommendations.
  • Water Generation via Atmospheric water harvesting, own your water supply reducing exposure to micro-plastics, contaminents and chemicals.
  • Attenuation Tanks for storm water, overflow, greywater recycling and rainwater harvesting

By implementing our water services, asset owners can significantly reduce their water footprint, save costs, and contribute to a more sustainable future. Contact us today to learn how we can help you optimize your water resources!
