Heat Loss Surveys

Our heat loss surveys are designed to provide a comprehensive assessment of your building's thermal performance, helping you identify areas of heat loss and improve energy efficiency. Our team of experts uses advanced tools and techniques to conduct thorough inspections and measurements, providing you with detailed insights into your building's heat loss patterns.

During a heat loss survey, we evaluate the building's insulation, identify thermal bridges, and assess the overall energy performance of your property. We provide you with a detailed report outlining our findings, along with recommendations for improving insulation, sealing gaps, and enhancing the overall energy efficiency of your building.

Our heat loss survey services include:

  • Thermal imaging to identify areas of heat loss
  • Assessment of insulation effectiveness
  • Identification of thermal bridges and air leaks
  • Detailed reporting with recommendations for improvements
  • Energy efficiency assessments and recommendations

By conducting a heat loss survey, you can gain valuable insights into your building's energy performance and take proactive steps to reduce energy consumption, lower utility bills, and enhance the comfort of your living or working environment. Contact us today to schedule a heat loss survey and start improving the energy efficiency of your property.
